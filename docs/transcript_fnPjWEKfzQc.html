MILE HIGH Skyscraper


Transcript

I would like to see us fulfill the dream of the early to mid-20th century and build a mile-high skyscraper. This was what people saw the development of skyscrapers going in the 30s and 40s and perhaps 50s. Frank Lloyd Wright designed this mile-high skyscraper called the Illinois, presumably back when Chicago was a metro of rising importance.

The technology for it exists and you haven't really seen anyone do it. Even these people who are willing to clearly build these giant white elephant projects, basically nobody has tried to go the distance and build a mile-high skyscraper. I would like to see us do it.

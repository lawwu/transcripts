A female podcast listener in Colorado writes in to ask this. "Hello Pastor John, with a heavy heart I ask. I think that's the main thing Paul's saying. We see this every time someone gets converted, and we see it in the Bible in 1 Peter 4 3, where it says, "For the time that has passed suffices for doing what the Gentiles want to do, living in sensuality, passions, drunkenness, orgies, drinking parties, lawless idolatries."

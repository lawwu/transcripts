How Do I Escape Regret over Hurt I’ve Caused?

Well, we all hurt others. That's part of what it means to be a sinner. And we live with the memories of the ways that we have hurt others. "Dear Pastor John, I have been reflecting recently and was reminded of the hurts I have inflicted upon people whom I have crossed paths with in the past.

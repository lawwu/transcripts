Why Twitter Will Change The World

When Erdogan was consolidating control of Turkey, Erdogan came out and he said, "I think Twitter is the primary challenge to the survival of any political regime." And I was like, "Okay, all the smart analysts all think this is worthless. And then a guy who's actually trying to keep control of a country is like, 'This is my number one threat.'" My conclusion was, "Erdogan is right, and all the smart Westerners are wrong." We're still pretty early in the long arc of social media.

The world in which five billion people are on the internet is still only a decade or so old. It took 200 years to fully play out the consequences of the printing press. We're still in the very early stages with these things.

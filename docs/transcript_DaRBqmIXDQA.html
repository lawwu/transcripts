US chose defeat over nukes


Transcript

The United States fought a number of wars on the periphery, beginning with Korea and then Vietnam and so forth, where we were willing to accept defeat rather than use our nuclear arsenal, always because behind those peripheral countries, if you will, was a major nuclear power, China, Soviet Union, whatever, and we didn't want to risk a nuclear war.

So at that level, deterrence really seemed to work for a long time. But there's been a change lately, and it's kind of terrifying, I find it, at least.

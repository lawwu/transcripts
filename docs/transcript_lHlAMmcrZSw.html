The use of Yahweh, I know it's one of the distinctive points of the LSB and maybe a lot of people would note that as something that's like a hallmark point but I think it's more than just a factual thing to note. For me reading the name Yahweh, God's personal covenant name, for instance in Psalm 23, I grew up memorizing it, "The Lord is my shepherd, I shall not want" and everything about it is true there's nothing taken away by using the word Lord, just reading it where it says, "Yahweh is my shepherd, I shall not want" but there's something about being able to know God's personal name.

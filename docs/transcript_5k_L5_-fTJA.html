Response to Sam Altman on Accelerating AI


Transcript

If you try to rouse your planet to give it any sense that it is in peril, there are the idiot disaster monkeys who are like, "Ooh, ooh, this sounds like if this is dangerous, it must be powerful, right? I'm going to be first to grab the poison banana." And what is one supposed to do?

Should one remain silent? Should one let everyone walk directly into the whirling razor blades? If you sent me back in time, I'm not sure I could win this, but maybe I would have some notion of, "Ah, if you calculate the message in exactly this way, then this group will not take away this message and you will be able to get this group of people to research on it without having this other group of people decide that it's excitingly dangerous and they want to rush forward on it." I'm not that smart.

I'm not that wise.

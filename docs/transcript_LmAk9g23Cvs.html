Here's an example from from my experience, see if this helps.

If I spank my son for blatant disobedience and he runs away from home because I spanked him, I may feel some remorse over the spanking, not in the sense that I disapprove of what I did, but in the sense that I feel some sorrow that the spanking was necessary and a part of a wise way of dealing with my son in this situation, and great sorrow that he ran away.

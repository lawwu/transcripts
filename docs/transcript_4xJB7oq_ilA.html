So to live by faith or to walk by faith or to do the works of faith means, one, the thing we aim at is shaped by God's Word, and it is good. Two, the strength to do it is the power of God, and we trust in it. Third, the aim of the act is make Christ look glorious.

Or to say it one more way, acting by faith means, one, what we do is shaped by the Word of God. How we do it is in the power of God, and the aim in doing it is the glory of God.

When I ask people what is their rich life, I have never, ever gotten someone saying, I want to sit around all week and optimize the price of printer paper. Not once, ever. When I ask them what is your rich life, they will always say things like spending more time with my family, traveling, new experiences, just going out with my partner and going out for a date night.

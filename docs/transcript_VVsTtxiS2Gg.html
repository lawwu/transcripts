How To Make Important Decisions


Transcript

Do you have a process for making big decisions in life or work? Well, there are decisions made under uncertainty and decisions made under certainty. So under certainty means you have a lot of data. Those are the easiest decisions and they actually are the least impactful because anybody can make those decisions.

The hardest decisions and the most impactful ones are the ones made under uncertainty. So that comes down to judgment. Well, judgment to me is decision-making under uncertainty, which means there's a bunch of luck involved, but some people show evidence of having better judgment than others.
